Our Roots

Crafting authentic valley memories

Explore Asia began with a simple belief: Kashmir should be experienced with absolute peace of mind, not rushed in large tourism groups.

We are headquartered locally in Srinagar. Our network of houseboats on Dal Lake and curated resorts in Gulmarg are hand-inspected for warmth, food hygiene, and panoramic vistas. By keeping our tours private, we customize every day around the local weather and your travel mood.
